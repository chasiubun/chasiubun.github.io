About Me

Ben Siu is a Board Certified Music Therapist in Orange County, California. He holds a B.M. in Music Therapy from University of the Pacific in Stockton, California. Ben took an internship in the Bay Area where he was hired full-time afterwards to continue providing music therapy services in hospice care before he moved back down to Orange County.

Ben initially found his pursuit of music through his parents who worked hard to get him music lessons on piano. Through church experiences, he came to learn multiple instruments such as guitar and voice.
