The costs for music therapy sessions are determined through the national averages for individual music therapy services while taking into account costs for other music-related occupations such as instrument or vocal private lessons.

Music Therapy has been able to be covered by Medicare under specific circumstances. This generally involves the prescription of music therapy services by a medical professional to directly assist with a clinical goal. However, the majority of services provided are paid through private pay options. Please consult with the physician of the patient/client regarding Medicare coverage.

Discounts are available for clients that book multiple weekly or biweekly sessions as well as for churches who have at least 3 clients receiving services in their membership.
